# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designers

### Role Summary
UX Designers create user experiences, workflows, and interfaces that meet usability standards and customer needs. They translate requirements into wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications that guide development.

### Responsibilities
- Design and iterate on UX/UI prototypes, wireframes, and mockups
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Collaborate with Product Managers to validate solutions meet user needs
- Work with Developers to ensure design feasibility and implementation fidelity
- Gather and incorporate user feedback into iterative design improvements
- Maintain design systems and style guides

### Goals
- Create intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce friction and improve user satisfaction metrics
- Ensure design consistency across the product

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Managers and Developers
- User research findings and usability test reports
- Prototype presentations and design specifications
- Iterative feedback sessions during development sprints

---

## Quality Assurance (QA) Engineers

### Role Summary
QA Engineers define, implement, and execute quality control processes to ensure product reliability and adherence to acceptance criteria. They validate features, identify defects, and advocate for quality standards throughout the delivery lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain comprehensive test plans and test cases
- Execute manual and automated testing across functional and non-functional requirements
- Identify, document, and track defects through resolution
- Collaborate with Developers to reproduce, diagnose, and verify bug fixes
- Ensure adherence to QA best practices and quality gates
- Validate features against acceptance criteria before release

### Goals
- Prevent defects from reaching production
- Maintain high product quality and reliability standards
- Reduce cycle time for bug identification and resolution

### Typical Communication
- Test plan reviews and testing status updates
- Bug reports and reproduction steps
- Feature readiness assessments with Project and Product Managers
- Quality metrics and test coverage reports

---

## DevOps Engineers

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers manage the build, deployment, and monitoring infrastructure that enables continuous delivery. They automate processes, ensure system reliability, and bridge the gap between development and operations.

### Responsibilities
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines and deployment automation
- Manage cloud infrastructure, containerization, and orchestration
- Implement monitoring, alerting, and observability solutions
- Ensure system reliability, scalability, and performance
- Automate deployment, rollback, and disaster recovery procedures
- Support incident response and root cause analysis

### Goals
- Enable fast, safe, and repeatable deployments
- Maximize system uptime and performance
- Reduce manual operational overhead through automation

### Typical Communication
- Deployment planning and release coordination with Developers
- Infrastructure changes and maintenance windows
- Incident response and post-mortems
- Collaboration with QA on test environment provisioning

---

## Security Leads

### Role Summary
Security Leads oversee security requirements, threat mitigation, and compliance throughout the project lifecycle. They proactively identify vulnerabilities, enforce security policies, and respond to security incidents.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct threat modeling and security architecture reviews
- Perform security-focused code reviews and penetration testing
- Enforce compliance with security policies and regulatory requirements
- Define security requirements and acceptance criteria
- Lead incident response and security remediation efforts
- Provide security training and guidance to development teams

### Goals
- Prevent security vulnerabilities from reaching production
- Ensure compliance with security standards and regulations
- Build security awareness and best practices into team culture

### Typical Communication
- Security assessments and threat model reviews
- Vulnerability reports and remediation plans
- Security requirement specifications
- Collaboration with Developers and Project Managers on risk mitigation

---

## Data Analysts

### Role Summary
Data Analysts analyze product metrics, user behavior, and business outcomes to provide actionable insights. They define KPIs, create reporting dashboards, and advise teams on data-driven improvements.

### Responsibilities
- Define product KPIs and success metrics aligned with business goals
- Build and maintain reporting dashboards and analytics infrastructure
- Conduct post-release metric reviews and A/B test analysis
- Translate data findings into actionable recommendations
- Advise Product and Project Managers on data-driven prioritization
- Work with Developers to instrument tracking and telemetry

### Goals
- Enable data-informed decision making across the organization
- Measure and communicate product impact and outcomes
- Identify opportunities for improvement through data analysis

### Typical Communication
- Metric reviews and data insights presentations
- Dashboard and reporting updates
- Collaboration with Product Managers on metric selection and interpretation
- Instrumentation requirements and tracking specifications for Developers

---

## Stakeholder Representatives

### Role Summary
Stakeholder Representatives advocate for the interests and needs of key stakeholder groups not directly on the core team. They ensure stakeholder perspectives are considered in planning and validate that deliverables meet broader organizational needs.

### Responsibilities
- Communicate stakeholder requirements and priorities to the project team
- Represent stakeholder interests in planning and decision-making meetings
- Provide feedback during milestone reviews and demonstrations
- Validate that deliverables align with stakeholder expectations
- Relay project updates and communicate changes back to stakeholder groups
- Escalate stakeholder concerns and risks to the project team

### Goals
- Ensure stakeholder needs are understood and addressed
- Maintain stakeholder alignment and satisfaction
- Bridge communication between project teams and broader organization

### Typical Communication
- Attendance at planning, review, and retrospective meetings
- Stakeholder feedback and requirement clarifications
- Status updates to stakeholder groups
- Escalation of concerns or changing requirements
